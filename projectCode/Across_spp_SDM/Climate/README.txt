

01_prism_bil2tif.R 
- Downloads prism monthly bil data (precipitation) for the following years: 1910-1950, 1985-2024 (needs 2025 when year is over?) 
- converts bil files to GeoTiff and exports to a designated folder 
- can adjust for annuals, normals, temperature etc. 


02_ppt_anomalies.R 
- takes spring prism data (March, April, May) and calcualtes annual averages, normals, and annual anomalies
